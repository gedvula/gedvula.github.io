My Choice For HBO-ICT :

♡⸝⸝ I chose the HBO-ICT programme because I have always been fascinated by technology and how it shapes the world around us.

⤷ For me, ICT is more than just coding—it’s about creating experiences. I love the creative side of web development, where design and functionality meet. Building a website is like designing a digital piece of art that people can interact with.

.ᐟ.ᐟ The HBO-ICT programme excites me because it combines technology with innovation. It gives me the tools to transform ideas into something real—whether it's a portfolio, an app, or a full website. I enjoy experimenting with layouts, colors, and code to make digital creations come alive.

Why ICT Suits Me :

  •   I like working on projects both individually and in teams
  •   I am curious about new technologies and innovations
  •   I want to build digital solutions that help people
  •   I enjoy mixing creativity with technical problem-solving
  •   I like designing websites that look great and work smoothly
  •   I’m curious about UX/UI design and front-end development
