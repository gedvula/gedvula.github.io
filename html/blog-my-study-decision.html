The Road to My Study Decision

Choosing the right study program has been an important step for me, and I’d like to share how I approached it and what I hope to achieve in the future.

My Background

In my prior education, I followed the Information Technology profile. This helped me develop a strong foundation in computer systems, software, and technical problem-solving. I’ve always been curious about how technology works and how it can be used to solve real-world problems. Although I haven’t studied at a university or a university of applied sciences before, I’m ready and motivated to take this next step in my education.

Study Choice Activities

To help guide my decision, I took part in several study choice activities. I completed a study choice test, joined an online information session, and had a chat with a student via hz.nl. The conversation with the student was especially helpful—it gave me a clear, honest view of what to expect from the program, the study environment, and how daily life as a student really looks. These activities helped me feel more confident that I’m choosing the right path.

My Future Plans

After completing the program, I see myself working as a web developer. I’ve always been fascinated by how websites and applications work, and I enjoy the challenge of writing code to solve problems. The idea of creating websites and tools that people use every day excites me, especially because web development is a field that’s always growing and changing. Over the next few years, I want to develop a strong understanding of both front-end and back-end web development. I aim to become skilled in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and popular frameworks like React or Node.js. I also want to learn how to build responsive, user-friendly applications that are both functional and visually appealing. At the same time, I hope to grow as a problem-solver and learn how to work well in a professional development team. In four years, I want to graduate with the technical and soft skills needed to make a real impact in the world of web development.
